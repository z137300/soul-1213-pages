Privacy Policy

App name: soul_1213
Website: https://z137300.github.io/soul-1213-pages/
Last updated: July 13, 2026

1. Overview

soul_1213 is a desktop application for Windows that helps users publish local MP4 videos to their own TikTok accounts using the official TikTok for Developers APIs (Login Kit and Content Posting API). This Privacy Policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what choices you have.

2. Information We Access

With your explicit authorization through TikTok OAuth, the app may access:

  • Basic profile information (scope: user.info.basic), such as open_id, to show login status in the app.
  • Content posting permissions (scopes: video.upload and/or video.publish) so you can upload drafts or publish videos you select.
  • OAuth tokens (access_token, refresh_token) issued by TikTok after you approve the authorization request.

The app does not request passwords. Authentication is handled entirely by TikTok.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not operate a backend server that stores your TikTok account data.
  • We do not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or location data.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.
  • We do not access your TikTok account except when you actively use login or publishing features.

4. How We Use Information

Information accessed through TikTok is used only to:

  • Complete OAuth login and maintain your authorized session on your device.
  • Upload or publish video files that you explicitly select on your computer.
  • Apply metadata you enter in the app (caption, hashtags, privacy level) to the post you choose to publish.
  • Display status messages and error logs locally to help you understand upload/publish progress.

5. TikTok API Integration

soul_1213 integrates with TikTok Login Kit and TikTok Content Posting API. When you click "Login TikTok", you are redirected to TikTok's authorization page. When you publish a video, the app sends the selected file and post metadata to TikTok using official API endpoints.

Your use of TikTok through this app is also subject to TikTok Terms of Service and TikTok Privacy Policy.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • OAuth tokens are stored locally on your computer (for example, in a tokens.json file next to the application).
  • App configuration (such as TikTok client credentials for your own developer app) is stored locally in config.json.
  • Selected video files remain on your device until you choose to upload or publish them.
  • You can remove stored tokens at any time by using "Log out" in the app or deleting the local token file.

You are responsible for keeping your device and local files secure.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. Video files and post metadata are transmitted only to TikTok's official servers when you initiate an upload or publish action.

8. User Control and Revocation

You can:

  • Decline or revoke OAuth permissions on TikTok's authorization screen.
  • Log out inside soul_1213 to delete locally stored tokens.
  • Revoke the app's access in TikTok app settings at any time.

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how soul_1213 handles data, contact the app owner via the repository or support channel associated with this project.
